About / Bio

Portrait

Sydney Silva is a multimedia interdisciplinary artist whose work aims to create an interactive experience. She loves to problem-solve and learn new skills, a quality developed through her 10 years in symphonic bands and her lifelong relationship with art.

Born in Santa Clara to first-generation Portuguese parents, she enjoys giving back to her community through festival events, window paintings, and chalk murals throughout the year.
