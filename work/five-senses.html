Five Senses

Sight Touch Taste & speech Smell Hearing

Five Senses, 2025, Procreate

A series on perception: sight, touch, speech, smell, and hearing—each piece pairs anatomical language with expressive color and texture. Each feature is based off of my own, and explores the five senses in a synesthetic way.
